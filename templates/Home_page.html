Primary Health Center in Permanent Campus of IIT Jodhpur received ISO Certification.

A Primary Health Center (PHC) has been started at the Permanent Campus of IIT Jodhpur from 15 June 2017. This fully equipped and self-sufficient facility is run by M/s. Goyal Hospital & Research Center Private Limited, Jodhpur. The facility is available to the residents of the Permanent Campus in Karwar. Presently, the following facilities available at the PHC:

  • 1. Qualified Medical Doctors,

  • 2. Qualified Medical Doctors,

  • 3. Paramedical Staff,

  • 4. Physiotherapy Unit,

  • 5. Pharmacy,

  • 6. 24 Hours Emergency Room, and

  • 7. an ICU Ambulance.

The PHC is ISO Certified.
